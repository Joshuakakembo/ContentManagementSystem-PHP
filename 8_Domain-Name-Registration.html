Why register your domain name?

A domain name is a unique name that identifies a website. For example, the domain name of the Hostalite is “hostalite.com” Each website has a domain name that serves as an address, which is used to access the website.

From a marketing and sales perspective, it is important to choose a domain name that will reflect the intent and purpose of your website or business. Your domain name will be one of the ways people recognize and evaluate your site when they are using a search engine. As a result choosing a name that will be marketable and can achieve success in search engine placement is vital.
